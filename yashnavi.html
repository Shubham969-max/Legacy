AICTE YASHASVI (YOUNG ACHIEVERS’ SCHOLARSHIP AND HOLISTIC ACADEMIC SKILLS VENTURE INITIATIVE) SCHEME 2024

Distribution of scholarships is based upon proportionate share of allotted degree seats in a stateThe All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) launched the Young Achievers’ Scholarship and Holistic Academic Skills Venture Initiative (YASHASVI) scheme for Civil, Chemical, Electrical, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering (CCEEM) degree and diploma students. The scheme was launched on June 07, 2024.

The scheme will benefit 5,000 students annually, with around 2,500 scholarships for degree students and 2,500 for diploma students. Degree students will receive Rs 18,000 per year for up to four years. Students enrolled in diploma will receive Rs 12,000 per year for up to three years. Reservation as per the respective state government reservation policy.

Eligibility Students should be admitted to the first year of degree/ diploma level course in any of the AICTE approved institutions of the respective year.

How to apply 1) The candidate fulfilling the eligibility conditions is required to apply online on National Scholarship Portal (NSP) after initiation of application process and notice to be published on AICTE website. 2) The host institution where the candidate is studying, required to verify the online application. 3) Department of Technical Education (DTE) of respective State/ UT will verify these applications as second level of scrutiny.

Things to remember State-wise distribution of scholarships is based upon proportionate share of allotted degree seats (in AICTE approved institutions) to that particular State/ UTs. The distribution of scholarships is based upon proportionate share of allotted diploma seats (in AICTE approved institutions) to that particular State/ UTs.
