PM Awas Yojana: After the decision to extend the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin till the year 2028-29, the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India has decided to conduct a survey again to identify the eligible people deprived of housing in rural areas. The dates of the survey will be announced by the Ministry soon. In this survey, many marking parameters have been amended. All homeless families, families living in one or two room houses with kutcha walls and kutcha roofs will be eligible for housing. Homeless families, destitute, beggars, manual scavengers, primitive tribal groups and legally freed bonded labourers will be eligible for housing. No eligible person should be left out in the survey: Keshav Maurya

Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya has instructed all the officials to conduct the upcoming survey in Uttar Pradesh in a correct and transparent manner. It has been instructed that during the survey, it should be ensured that no eligible beneficiary is deprived of inclusion in the list. There should not be any kind of negligence in the survey work. Even before the date of the survey is received from the Government of India, Rural Development Commissioner GS Priyadarshi has issued guidelines to all the District Magistrates for necessary preparations. Loan is available from the bank Under the PM Awas Yojana, people are given houses by giving loans. Applications are invited for housing to homeless families living as tenants. Beneficiaries have to deposit 10 percent contribution amount and participate in the lottery to get the house allotted in their name. Some documents are necessary documents to get a loan from the bank. After the house is allotted under the PM Awas Yojana, you can take a loan from the bank. For this

-Photocopy of allotment letter -Photocopy of PAN card -Photocopy of Aadhar card -Photocopy of bank pass book -Affidavit -6 months bank statement -Salary certificate -3 passport size photos -Rent agreement -Photocopy of receipt of deposited amount is mandatory.
